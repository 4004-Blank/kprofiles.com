MASSIVE TWO Members Profile: MASSIVE TWO Facts

MASSIVE TWO(매시브투) is a South Korean girl group debuted on February 20, 2020 under BSS Entertainment. In pre-debut group was consist of 6 members: Tiangjing, Dorothy, Shakira, Fasha, Julie, and Nina. In 2020, Hyeyeon was added to the group and now it is consists of 7 members.

MASSIVE TWO Fandom Name: Immense
MASSIVE TWO Official Fan Color: Dark Chromatic Green

MASSIVE TWO Current Dorm Arrangement:
– Hyeyeon, Shakira, & Julie
– Tiangjing & Fasha
– Dorothy & Nina
